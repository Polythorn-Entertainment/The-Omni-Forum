Community Standards

Rules of OmniForum

These rules are here to keep the forum sharp, safe, and worth returning to. Staff may remove content or accounts that undermine the community even if a situation is not spelled out word-for-word below.

1. Treat people like people

No harassment, hate speech, stalking, threats, dogpiling, intimidation, or personal attacks. Disagreement is welcome; cruelty is not.

2. Keep it legal and safe

Do not post illegal content, doxxing, malware, stolen material, fraud schemes, or anything designed to exploit other users. Security discussion is fine; instructions for active abuse are not.

3. No spam or low-effort flooding

Do not mass-post, repost the same topic, drop meaningless filler, or use the forum primarily for self-promotion. Share your work when it adds value and participate like a member, not an ad slot.

4. Use the right spaces

Post in the closest matching section, write clear titles, and keep tags honest. Threads may be moved, merged, pinned, locked, or removed to keep the forum readable.

5. Respect privacy

Do not post private conversations, personal contact details, hidden account data, or identifiable information about anyone without permission.

6. Keep accounts honest

Do not impersonate staff or other members. Ban evasion, alt-account abuse, vote/like manipulation, or gaming role systems may lead to account restrictions or removal.

7. Moderator decisions

Staff may edit, remove, or lock content to protect the forum. If you think something was handled unfairly, use the contact form instead of escalating publicly inside threads.
